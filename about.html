BEHIND THE PROJECT



             We the group of three people always wanted to create a 

             photogrpahy page as photogrpahy is our passion and now it 
             
             came our profession.

            The project began as a very simple idea; over the course

            of a year, we wanted to create a series of portraits We 

            are a team of researchers, editors, writers and creatives united
             
            by a shared goal of building more equitable resources for the study of art

           our little idea has grown into a wonderful celebration of talented

           photographers  who deserve this wonderful  recognition.

           We started BLINK with 0.1% percent  knowledge and it went to great
           
           extent. so here we give oppurtunities to many  people who are
           
           passionate about PHOTOGRAPHY.There are various kind of 
       
           photographies and we are running this in all various kind of 
        
           photographies.
        

           

         OUR INSPIRATION

        She is Annie Leibovitz
        Her style is characterized by  
        carefully staged settings, superb  
        lighting, and use of vivid colour.
        she is an inspiration for women who
        have passion on photogrpahy but not 
        stepping into this field.
        we got inspired by her work and we 
        started this as she also started this
        profession from scratch and now it became
        huge success.Though she is an american 
        photographer she is inspiring many people
        of different continents.
